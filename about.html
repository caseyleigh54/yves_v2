HELLO FRIENDS!

Hi, I'm Yves (pronounced Eve).

I am so glad you’re here! I’m looking forward to getting to know you better, but first I want you to know a little bit about me and how I got to where I am now.

Let’s start at the beginning... From a young age, I didn’t really like food. The term ‘fussy eater’ is a massive understatement. I ate no vegetables, no meat, no dairy… my diet consisted of pasta, pizza, peanut butter and fruit. I was conscious of my health but struggled to find food that I liked. This led me to learning more about the nutrients in food and helped me to find foods that I liked but were also good for me. This, in combination with my love of biology and chemistry, started my passion for nutrition and dietetics. I studied at La Trobe University in Melbourne, Victoria and obtained my Bachelor of Applied Science and Masters in Dietetic Practice. Today I am an Accredited Practicing Dietitian (APD).

I have helped many patients with a range of different medial conditions, eat right and not let their illness define them. I have a particular interest on the link between nutrition and disease. It is an ever-growing and ever-changing area which can make it difficult to navigate for the everyday person. There is so much false and misinterpreted information out there, which makes it hard to know what to eat for your individual needs.

As a dietitian, I am invested in client centred healthcare, based on medical nutrition therapy. Most importantly I am specialised in the development of appropriate health plans tailored and specific to YOUR needs. I not only want to up your veggie intake and teach you how to cook up a delicious meal, I want to help you explore your relationship with food and guide you to live a happier, healthier and more colourful life.

Now that you know a little bit about me, I can’t wait to learn more about you!

Kind Regards,

Yves Charlesworth
